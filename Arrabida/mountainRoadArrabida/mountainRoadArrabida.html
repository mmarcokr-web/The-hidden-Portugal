Portinho da Arrabida

Arrábida, Portugal

Viewpoint of N-379 of Portinho da Arrábida

A stunning cove famous for calm, turquoise waters and crystal-clear visibility, making it a paradise for diving and snorkeling. It features the historic Fort of Santa Maria, which now houses an oceanographic museum.

⚠️ Parking Warning: Extremely limited during summer and good weather weekends
