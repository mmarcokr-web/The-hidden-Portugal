Santa Margarida Cave

Arrábida, Portugal

The Secret Chapel by the Sea

In the Arrábida mountains, this cave houses a 17th-century chapel and an exit to the ocean.

⚠️ Travel Tip: On hot days, going up the stairs can be very hard. This visit is best enjoyed on cooler days to avoid exhaustion.
